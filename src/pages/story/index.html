Since our passionate founder Sansarmaa Gantumur opened her first Campos café in Ulaanbaatar in 2021, we’ve been on a mission to help everyone love great coffee as much as we do. We believe in coffee’s ability to inspire genuine optimism and joy. From your very first sip things magically get better; conversations flow freely, grand ideas flourish and new connections feel like old friends.
